How To Play:

    Get 6 chances to guess the 5 letter word!
    The colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
    If a tile lights up GREEN, your letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
    If a tile lights up YELLOW, your letter is in the word but is in the wrong spot.
    If a tile lights up RED, your letter is not in the word.

Tries remaining: 6
