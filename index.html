Word Guesser

Rules:

Guess the word in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word.
  • The color of your tiles will change to reflect your accuracy.

    • Your tile will turn green if your letter is in the right spot.

    Your tile will turn yellow if your letter is in the word, but in the wrong spot.

    Your tile will turn red if your letter is not in the word.

    Tries remaining: 6
